Our Medical Services

Comprehensive healthcare solutions tailored to your needs — available at your home or in our clinic

General Medicine

Our general medicine service provides comprehensive primary care for patients of all ages. From routine checkups to diagnosis and treatment of acute and chronic conditions, our experienced physicians are here to keep you and your family healthy.

  • Comprehensive physical examinations
  • Diagnosis and treatment of acute illnesses
  • Management of chronic conditions
  • Preventive care and health screenings
  • Vaccinations and immunizations
Book Consultation

Home Medical Check Ups

Can't make it to the clinic? Our medical team comes to you. We offer comprehensive home visit services for patients who prefer the comfort and convenience of being examined in their own environment.

  • Full physical examinations at your home
  • Vital signs monitoring (BP, temperature, pulse)
  • Blood sugar and cholesterol testing
  • Health assessments and risk evaluations
  • Flexible scheduling to fit your routine
Schedule Home Visit

Nursing Services for Elderly & Palliative Care

Compassionate, professional nursing care for elderly patients and those requiring palliative support. Our trained nurses provide comfort, dignity, and quality of life for patients with chronic or terminal illnesses.

  • 24/7 skilled nursing care at home
  • Palliative and end-of-life care with dignity
  • Bedside care and personal hygiene assistance
  • Pain and symptom management
  • Emotional and psychological support for families
  • Post-hospitalization recovery care
Request Nursing Care

Orthopedic Services & Orthotics Supply

Specialized care for bone, joint, and muscle conditions. Our orthopedic specialists provide expert diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation services, plus quality orthotic devices to support your mobility.

  • Diagnosis and treatment of fractures, sprains, and arthritis
  • Joint pain management and rehabilitation
  • Custom orthotics: braces, supports, and insoles
  • Mobility aids: crutches, walkers, canes
  • Post-surgical orthopedic rehabilitation
  • Pediatric and geriatric orthopedic care
Consult Orthopedic Specialist

Deliveries of Prescription & Medical Appliances

We bring your medications and medical supplies right to your doorstep. No more waiting in pharmacy lines—simply send us your prescription, and we'll deliver promptly and safely.

  • Prescription medication delivery across Uganda & Kenya
  • Medical equipment: blood pressure monitors, glucose meters, nebulizers
  • Wound care supplies and dressings
  • Mobility aids and orthopedic supports
  • Discreet and timely delivery service
  • Refill reminders and subscription options
