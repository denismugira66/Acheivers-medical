Our Medical Services

General Consultation

Our general consultation service provides comprehensive primary care for patients of all ages. Whether you're dealing with a sudden illness, need a routine checkup, or have concerns about your health, our experienced doctors are here to help.

  • Same-day appointments available
  • Comprehensive physical examinations
  • Diagnosis and treatment of acute conditions
  • Referrals to specialists when needed

Chronic Disease Management

Living with a chronic condition requires ongoing support and expert guidance. Our chronic disease management program helps you take control of your health with personalized care plans.

  • Diabetes management and education
  • Hypertension and heart health monitoring
  • Respiratory condition support (asthma, COPD)
  • Regular follow-ups and medication management

Comprehensive Health Checkups

Prevention is better than cure. Our comprehensive health checkups help detect potential health issues early, when they're most treatable.

  • Executive wellness packages
  • Basic health screening
  • Laboratory tests and diagnostics
  • Personalized health reports and recommendations
